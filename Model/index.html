Логическая модель

  1. customers
    • customer_id (INT, Primary Key)
    • first_name (VARCHAR(50))
    • last_name (VARCHAR(50))
    • email (VARCHAR(100))
    • phone (VARCHAR(20))
    • address (TEXT)
  2. categories
    • category_id (INT, Primary Key)
    • category_name (VARCHAR(100))
    • parent_category_id (INT, Foreign Key to categories.category_id)
  3. suppliers
    • supplier_id (INT, Primary Key)
    • company_name (VARCHAR(100))
    • contact_name (VARCHAR(100))
    • phone (VARCHAR(20))
  4. products
    • product_id (INT, Primary Key)
    • product_name (VARCHAR(255))
    • description (TEXT)
    • price (DECIMAL(10,2))
    • category_id (INT, Foreign Key to categories.category_id)
    • supplier_id (INT, Foreign Key to suppliers.supplier_id)
  5. orders
    • order_id (INT, Primary Key)
    • customer_id (INT, Foreign Key to customers.customer_id)
    • order_date (DATETIME)
    • status (ENUM('обрабатывается', 'оплачен', 'отправлен', 'доставлен'))
    • total_amount (DECIMAL(10,2))
  6. order_items
    • order_item_id (INT, Primary Key)
    • order_id (INT, Foreign Key to orders.order_id)
    • product_id (INT, Foreign Key to products.product_id)
    • quantity (INT)
    • unit_price (DECIMAL(10,2))
  7. inventory
    • inventory_id (INT, Primary Key)
    • product_id (INT, Foreign Key to products.product_id)
    • quantity (INT)
    • last_restocked (DATETIME)
